FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our premium epoxy flooring services.

Most single and double garages are completed within 2 days. Day 1 involves surface preparation and base coat application, Day 2 is the top coat. You can typically walk on it within 12 hours and drive on it within 24-48 hours, depending on weather conditions.

Polyaspartic is a superior technology to traditional epoxy. It cures much faster (allowing 2-day completion), is more flexible (won't crack with concrete movement), and is highly resistant to UV yellowing, making it perfect for the Gold Coast's harsh sun. It's also more chemical and heat resistant.

Yes! All our residential installations come with a 15-year product warranty against peeling and delamination when installed on sound concrete. Our workmanship is guaranteed for 3 years.

We add anti-slip additives to the top coat as standard to ensure the floor remains safe even when wet. This makes it suitable for driveways, pool surrounds, and areas prone to moisture. We can adjust the slip resistance based on your specific needs.

Our preparation is comprehensive and includes diamond grinding to open the concrete pores, thorough cleaning, crack repair (where possible), and moisture testing. Proper preparation is 80% of a successful installation, which is why we never cut corners here.
